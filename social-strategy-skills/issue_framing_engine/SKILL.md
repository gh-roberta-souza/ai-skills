# Issue Framing Engine

Communication frameworks for issue presentation.

## When to use

- Issue framing strategy
- Message angle selection
- Campaign positioning
- Public interest communication

## Framing models

- **Rights frame**: fundamental rights, entitlements
- **Accountability frame**: who is responsible, who must act
- **Systemic failure frame**: broken system, structural problem
- **Public interest frame**: collective good, community impact

## Rules

| Rule | Value |
|------|-------|
| Prefer rights frame for public services | true |
