# Distribution Strategy Engine

Plans content distribution and amplification.

## When to use

- Distribution planning
- Amplification strategy
- Reach optimization
- Community activation

## Distribution methods

- **Organic distribution**: feed, explore, hashtags
- **Influencer amplification**: partner outreach, co-creation
- **Community sharing**: save, share, repost

## Rules

| Rule | Value |
|------|-------|
| Prioritize shareability | true |

## Integration

Invoked by `social_strategy_orchestrator` during Stage 6 (distribution).
