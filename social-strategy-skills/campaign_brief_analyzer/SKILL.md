# Campaign Brief Analyzer

Transforms briefs into strategic inputs.

## When to use

- Campaign kickoff
- Brief interpretation
- Strategy extraction
- Message definition

## Analysis dimensions

- **Problem definition**: core issue to address
- **Audience definition**: who needs to hear this
- **Desired action**: what should the audience do
- **Emotional context**: emotional stakes and resonance

## Outputs

- Core message
- Campaign goal
- Narrative angle

## Framework

**PAIA** (Problem, Audience, Impact, Action)

| Dimension | Extract |
|-----------|---------|
| Problem | What is broken or unjust |
| Audience | Who cares, who can act |
| Impact | Real-world consequences |
| Action | Clear next step |

## Integration

Invoked by `social_strategy_orchestrator` during Stage 1 (brief analysis).
