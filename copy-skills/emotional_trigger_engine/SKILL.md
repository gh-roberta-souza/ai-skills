# Emotional Trigger Engine

Emotional drivers for engagement with credibility preservation.

## When to use

- Advocacy campaigns
- Awareness content
- Impact storytelling
- Community mobilization

## Triggers

- **Injustice**: unfairness, systemic failure
- **Urgency**: time-sensitive stakes
- **Empathy**: shared experience, identification
- **Accountability**: who is responsible

## Rules

| Rule | Value |
|------|-------|
| Avoid sensationalism | true |
| Maintain factual tone | true |
