# Attention Capture Engine

First-second attention mechanics for scroll-stopping hooks.

## When to use

- Creating social media openings
- Headline generation
- Carousel first-slide copy
- Any content requiring immediate attention

## Hook types

- **Big number**: quantified impact or scale
- **Shocking fact**: unexpected statistic or revelation
- **Contradiction**: tension between expectation and reality
- **Bold statement**: declarative claim
- **Urgent problem**: immediate stakes

## Rules

| Rule | Value |
|-----|-------|
| Max words | 10 |
| Must create curiosity | true |

## Examples

- "71 complaints. Zero solutions."
- "Water is a right. Not a privilege."

## Integration

Invoked by `copy_ai_orchestrator` during Stage 2 (attention capture).
