# Memetic Phrase Generator

Creates phrases that spread and repeat.

## When to use

- Campaign slogans
- Quotable moments
- Carousel takeaways
- Shareable soundbites

## Rules

| Rule | Value |
|------|-------|
| Max words | 6 |
| Must be repeatable | true |

## Examples

- "Water is a right."
- "No water. No silence."
