# Water & Sanitation Bahia Knowledge

Domain knowledge for water supply and sanitation regulation in Bahia, Brazil.

|-----------|-------|

## Authoritative sources

### Federal law

- Lei 11.445/2007
- Lei 14.026/2020
- Lei 8.987/1995

### State law

- Lei Estadual 11.172/2008
- Decreto Estadual 12.212/2010

### Regulatory body

- AGERSA

### Regulatory instruments

- Resolução AGERSA 002/2017

### National regulator

- ANA (Agência Nacional de Águas e Saneamento Básico)

## Core principles

| Principle | Description | Source |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Service continuity | Water supply services must be continuous and regular | Lei 11.445/2007 Art. 2 |
| Service quality | Service must be adequate, efficient, safe and updated | Lei 11.445/2007 Art. 2 |
| Universal access | National policy targets universal water access by 2033 | Lei 14.026/2020 |
| Regulatory oversight | AGERSA is responsible for supervising water services in Bahia | Lei Estadual 11.172/2008 |

All principles: **verification_status: verified**
