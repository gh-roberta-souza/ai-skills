# Legal Risk (Water Claims)

Evaluates legal exposure in public communication about water and sanitation services.

## Risk levels

### Low risk

- Citing legislation
- Citing regulatory processes
- Reporting documented complaints
- Describing service interruptions

### Medium risk

- Stating regulatory non-compliance
- Interpreting service failures

**Mitigation:** cite sources, avoid attribution of intent.

### High risk

- Corruption allegations
- Accusations against individuals
- Claims of criminal conduct

**Rule:** prohibit unless supported by judicial decision.

## Communication guidelines

### Preferred frames

- Right to water
- Service quality
- Regulatory oversight

### Avoid frames

- Corruption narrative
- Conspiracy narrative

## Integration

Use after `water_crisis_evidence_analysis` (or `regulatory_compliance_water_services` when evidence analysis is not used). Final domain layer before OSINT, strategy, copy, and design. Ensures safe, factual, defensible public communication.
